About Us

Formula for Success is a nonprofit organization dedicated to introducing young minds to the exciting world of mathematics. Led by high school students, we provide free educational resources and programs for elementary to middle school aged students. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to explore their mathematical potential without barriers. Through our programs, we aim to build confidence and curiosity in young learners, setting them on a path to not only academic success, but also a love for mathematics. Join us in nurturing the next generation of students who will shape the future with their innovative ideas.
